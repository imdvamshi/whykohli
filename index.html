Why Virat Kohli?

In December 2006, when Virat Kohli was just 18 years old and on the brink of breaking into the Indian cricket team, he suffered a devastating loss - his father, Prem Kohli, passed away due to a stroke. Despite this immense personal tragedy, Kohli displayed extraordinary resilience and commitment to the game.
Kohli's performance in that match was nothing short of remarkable. He displayed incredible focus and mental strength, scoring 90 runs in the first innings and an unbeaten 59 in the second innings. This not only showcased his talent as a cricketer but also his ability to thrive under pressure and overcome personal challenges.
"Inspired by Virat Kohli's resilience and dedication, this website celebrates his remarkable journey, showcasing his triumphs, challenges, and enduring legacy in the world of cricket."
